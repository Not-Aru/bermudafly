How We Fly

From check-in to landing, this is how we fly.

  • Check-In

    If you're one of our Frequent Flyers, say hello to our priority check-in lane. We take care of your bags for you, so everything is hassle-free.

    One carry-on bag for each passenger is allowed. Please notify an attendant if you need any assistance or accomodations.
