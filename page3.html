As a regional manager, you're responsible for the staffing, profit, performance and other operations at each Bermuda location you manage. Supervisors, store managers, or general managers can eventually become regional managers with leadership and industry experience.

Bermuda requires that you hold a Bachelor's degree in business or a related field. Regional managers must have strong decision-making and problem-solving skills. If you are a prospective regional manager, you must also be able to multitask and work under conditions that are high in stress. Though you should already be experts in your industry, regional managers should demonstrate an ongoing knowledge of the industry complete with continuing education.
